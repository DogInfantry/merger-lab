# MERGER LAB — India M&A Deal Room

An M&A accretion/dilution engine for Indian public markets. Pick a listed
acquirer and target, structure the consideration inside the **RBI 2026
acquisition-finance guardrails** and **SEBI Takeover Code**, and download the
two artifacts that matter: an **IC memo PDF** and a **linked Excel model**.

- Full source, methodology and hand-checked tests:
  **https://github.com/DogInfantry/merger-lab**
- Sample deal rooms (memo + Excel) live in the repo's `samples/`.

Illustrative analysis on public data (yfinance). Not investment advice.
